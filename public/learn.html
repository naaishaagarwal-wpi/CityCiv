How to Use CityCiv:

CityCiv is designed to make it easy for you to stay informed and engaged with your local government. Here's how to get started:

1. Explore the Dashboard: Check out the latest news, events, and updates from your local community! Here, you can also make posts of your own! Share your thoughts, and engage with other community members.

2. Attend Town Meetings: View upcoming meetings and watch recordings of past meetings to stay informed about local issues.

3. Manage Your Account: Update your profile, adjust your notification preferences, and customize your experience.
